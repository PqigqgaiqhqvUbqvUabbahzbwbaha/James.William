VinFast – Driving the Electric Future

Vietnam's automotive brand making global waves

About VinFast

VinFast is a Vietnamese electric vehicle (EV) manufacturer, part of the Vingroup conglomerate. Founded in 2017, VinFast aims to revolutionize sustainable transportation with high-quality, affordable electric cars, buses, and scooters for global markets.

Popular Models

VinFast’s main lineup includes electric SUVs such as the VF 8 and VF 9. These models come equipped with modern design, long battery range, smart infotainment systems, and advanced driver-assist features.

Global Expansion

VinFast is expanding globally, with production facilities in Vietnam and plans to build a factory in North Carolina, USA. The company has entered markets such as the U.S., Canada, Europe, and continues to grow its global presence in the EV industry.

Commitment to Sustainability

VinFast focuses on producing electric vehicles to reduce emissions and promote clean energy. With rapid development and innovation, VinFast is committed to building a sustainable future through green mobility solutions.
