About VinFast

VinFast is a Vietnamese EV brand founded by Vingroup. Since 2017, it has grown into a global player, focusing on electric cars, buses, and scooters.

Key Models

VF 8 and VF 9 are VinFast’s flagship electric SUVs, featuring smart technology, long-range batteries, and sustainable materials.

Global Expansion

VinFast is building a factory in North Carolina, USA, and has launched products in North America and Europe to compete in the global EV market.

Green Vision

VinFast’s mission is to drive the transition to clean energy by creating reliable, eco-friendly vehicles for everyone.
