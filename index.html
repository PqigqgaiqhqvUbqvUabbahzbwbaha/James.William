About VinFast

VinFast, founded in 2017 by Vingroup, is Vietnam's first major car manufacturer. It has quickly gained global attention for its electric vehicles (EVs) designed for sustainability and innovation.

Popular Models

VinFast’s flagship EVs include the VF8 and VF9 — fully electric SUVs featuring advanced technology, long-range batteries, and modern styling.

Global Presence

VinFast is expanding to global markets including the U.S., Canada, and Europe. It is also building a manufacturing facility in North Carolina, USA.

Commitment to Green Mobility

With a strong focus on electric mobility, VinFast aims to lead the global transition toward cleaner, greener transportation.
